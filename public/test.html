Typing Tutorial

Proper Typing Position

Home Row Position

Place your fingers on the home row keys (ASDF for left hand, JKL; for right hand). Your thumbs should hover over the space bar.

Posture

Sit up straight with your feet flat on the floor. Keep your wrists slightly elevated and your elbows at a 90-degree angle.

Improving Speed & Accuracy

1

Start Slow

Focus on accuracy first. Speed will naturally improve as you become more comfortable.

2

Practice Regularly

Short daily practice sessions are more effective than occasional long sessions.

3

Use All Fingers

Avoid the "hunt and peck" method. Train yourself to use all fingers for maximum efficiency.
